Step 1

All the Google offices outside the USA sent their money to Ireland


Why do they do that?

Because of the income taxes rates:

  • Irland - 12.5%
  • USA - 30%
  • Great Britan - 28%

So 90% of all revenue generated outside the USA comes to Google Ireland Limited

but Google Ireland Limited doesn't pay tax from this ammount

It has to pay for technology licence to another Google Company - Google Ireland Holdings.

Why so complicated? Well...

Google Ireland Limited (the first company) has now bigger costs and can deduct it from the income.

Lower income means lower tax.

The second company, Google Ireland Holdings, the owner of technology also operates in Irland.

Will it pay taxes? Well, no!

It is only formally set up in Irland, this company is a tax resident in Bermudas.

But Irland tax law forbides tax deductions from licence costs when the second company is not from UE.

Fortunatelly Google does not have this restrictions in Holland

Google has another branch in Holand so it is saved

Google Netherlands B.V. is the connector between two Irland companes.

This is why we call it ‘Double Irish with a Dutch Sandwich’
